# OpenELIS-Global-2 Overview

## Introduction

OpenELIS-Global-2 is the primary open-source repository of OpenELIS Global, a comprehensive Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) designed to support clinical and public health laboratories at both facility and national levels. The platform is built to automate and streamline laboratory workflows, improve data accuracy, and enhance interoperability with other health information systems.

## What OpenELIS Global Does

OpenELIS Global manages and optimizes key laboratory operations, including:

* Patient registration and sample tracking
* Test ordering and result reporting
* Quality assurance and control workflows
* Laboratory analyzer integration
* Standards-based interoperability with other health systems

The system supports configurable workflows for diverse laboratory settings, ensuring flexibility while maintaining compliance with international laboratory standards.
